Hexagon Hall

Was established in 2000

The Hall has a unique hexagonal architecture with different areas being utilized for different purposes. It is a multipurpose hall with a seating capacity of well over 100. The hall is fully air conditioned. It can be transformed to facilitate Seminars, Symposiums, Conferences and Exhibitions.

school library

Was built in 1980

The school library is part of our school’s teaching and learning environment. It provides resources and services that support students, staff, families and whānau. Your library’s resources, physical space, and staff have tremendous potential to make a difference to students’ achievement, educational equity, and their social and emotional well-being.

school auditorium

Inter School Quiz On Sports
Garden High School emerged the Overall Runners-up of the event.

Students of Classes X and XI participated in the Inter-School Sports Quiz GOLDEN GAUNTLET, sponsored by the Debanjan Sen Foundation. The competition was held on a virtual platform from 29 June to 4 July 2021. 19 schools participated in this event. The competition comprised 3 quizzes: General Sports (TOUCHDOWN), Cricket (BEAMER), and Football (NUTMEG) Biswaroop Chowdhury (XIA) was the Team Captain and the team members were, Monoshij Ghosh (XIA), Ishan Burman (XIC), Samriddha Nandi (10A), Shounak Mazumdar (XE) and Shiladitya Banerjee (XE). Shounak Mazumdar, Biswaroop Chowdhury and Shiladitya Banerjee were placed Second in the Football Quiz (Nutmeg). The boys showed their prowess in the face of stiff competition. In a nail- biting finish they did the school proud.

Crescendo 2021
A big round of applause for all the winners. Congratulations!

Crescendo was an online business fest hosted and conducted by Lilavatibai Podar School, Mumbai. It was held on 10 April 2021. One had to apply the knowledge acquired in economics and commerce along with current affairs and reasoning to participate in events like real life monopoly, stock exchange and creating brands. Students of Garden High School, Class 12 Commerce, participated in the fest and won the first position as overall National ISC Winners amongst 25 other participating schools.   • New School Canteen

    School Canteen A nutritious diet is essential to healthy brain development in children, which is critical to good learning. Our school kitchen team prepares fresh meals every day on site. As well as a range of wholesome, nutritious and delicious snacks and meals for all, the team also provides for students with special dietary needs. There is a daily morning snack, afternoon snack, and at lunchtime a soup starter and two main courses, one of each being a vegetarian option. Parents are provided with a menu in advance of each week and are able to select the food they would like their children to be served each day. Having good eating habits and a balanced diet supports children’s health and wellbeing, and minimises the risk of illness. Eating habits developed in the early years are likely to have a lifelong influence. Fruit is naturally available daily. The school works with the best suppliers that meet stringent criteria and are certified for importing raw materials into the school kitchen.
